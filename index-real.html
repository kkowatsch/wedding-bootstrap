Our Story

In mid-January 2014, I was sitting in my college class waiting for a test to begin. While waiting, I was explaining to some of my peers that I was looking for a place to live during my last year of school. As fate would have it, my close friend mentioned that he had an extra room available in the house he would be renting with his friends. Just then, the professor walked in and started handing out the infamous tests. I noticed I had nothing to write with, so I asked my friend if I could borrow a pencil. The test began and ended as unremarkable as a test could be.

After class, I started towards the doors that led to residence when I realized I was still holding onto the pencil. At the time, it was important that I give it back immediately, so I made my way to the back of the school restaurant where my friend was standing with a small group of people. As soon as he saw me, my friend began the introductions which is how Justin and I first met. Naturally, Justin and I spent the next hour getting to know each other. I discovered he would also be moving into the same house that my friend had mentioned earlier. When it came time to leave, we said our goodbyes and did not speak again for some time.

Fast-forward to Friday night a few weeks later. I had intended to stay-in but on a whim, I decided to meet up with some friends at the only night club in town. As soon as we stepped inside, I noticed Justin was on the other side of the room with a group of his friends. He was at the club to celebrate his birthday. We must have both done a lot of celebrating that night because every time we bumped into each other, Justin would ask for my number and I would happily oblige.

The next day we started talking and could not stop. Eventually, Justin asked me if I would go on a date with him and I of course said yes. We were inseparable for the rest of the semester. On April 14, 2014, Justin officially asked me to be his girlfriend. The following September, we became roommates in the same house that first brought us together.

Fate really is a funny thing. Looking back, there were so many small decisions that led to our beginning. Now here we are, eight years later, about to embark on the next chapter of our life together. We could not be happier and it's all thanks to a pencil, a house and a random Friday night.
