Our Story

In January 2014, I was sitting in one of my college classes waiting for an unfortunate test to begin. While waiting, I was explaining to some of my peers that I had no where to live for the following year of school and I was beginning to grasp at straws. Luckily, a close friend, lets call him M, mentioned that he had one extra room available in the house he would be moving into with some of his friends. It was fate! Unfortunately, I had to wait to respond to M because the professor chose that moment to start handing out the tests. Now i've always been a tad forgetful, so of course I forgot to bring writing utensils. Thankfully M had a spare pencil for me to borrow. The test began and ended as unremarkable as any test could be.

After class, I started walking towards the doors that led to residence when I realized I was still holding onto that borrowed pencil. I cannot tell you why but it was very important that I return the pencil to it's proper owner immediately, so I made my way to the back of the school restaurant where M was standing with a small group of people. As soon as he saw me, M began the introductions of his friends.There was only one guy in particular who stood out to me. His name was Justin and he was cute, funny, interesting and he kept trying to convince me that he was allergic to all fruits and vegetables. I was instantly hooked. I found out that he would be one of the people moving into the same house that M had mentioned earlier. We spent the next hour getting to know each other before we had to leave. Over the next few weeks, we bumped into each other infrequently and never said more than a friendly greeting.

Fast forward to a random Friday night. I was intending to stay-in for the night but on a whim and a little prodding from some friends, I ended up at the only night club in town. It was fairly busy that night so it took us quite some time to get inside. When we finally made it inside and to the bar area, I was happily surprised to run into Justin. Apparently he was at the club to celebrate his birthday. The rest of the night gets a little hazy. We must have both done a lot of celebrating that night because every time we bumped into each other, Justin would ask for my number and I would happily oblige. I ended up in his phone six or seven times.

The next day we started talking and could not stop. Eventually, Justin asked me if I would go on a date with him and I of course said yes. We were inseparable for the rest of the semester. On April 14, 2014, Justin officially asked me to be his girlfriend. The following September, we became roommates in the same house that first brought us together.

Fate really is a funny thing. Looking back, there were so many small decisions that led to our beginning. Now here we are, eight years later, about to embark on the next chapter of our life together. We could not be happier and it's all thanks to a pencil, a house and a random Friday night.
